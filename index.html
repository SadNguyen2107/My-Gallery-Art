Painting

Painting is the practice of applying pigment to a surface using a brush, palette knife, or other tools. It can be representational or abstract.

Sculpture

Sculpture is a three-dimensional art form created by shaping or combining hard materials, typically stone, metal, or wood. It can be representational or abstract.

Photography

Photography is the art of capturing light with a camera to create images. It can be used for various purposes, including art, documentation, and communication.
